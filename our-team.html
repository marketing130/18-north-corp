Managing Partners

Roman Brailovski

Managing Partner & Co-Founder

A versatile professional, Roman has led and managed multi-billion-dollar real estate portfolios for major financial institutions and top Canadian real estate firms, including Cadillac Fairview and the Great Gulf Group. Prior to co-founding 18 North, Roman served as EVP of Operations at CAPREIT (Canada's largest publicly-traded multifamily owner & operator), where he was directly responsible for a portfolio of over 60,000 units, valued at more than $16 billion and serviced by a workforce of over 850 professionals across two continents.

Roman's accomplishments include building an operations service platform for over $5 billion of commercial assets across Canada and the US, and the strategic development of office, retail, life sciences, and industrial assets across North America. As a Managing Partner of 18 North, Roman co-led the successful purchase, repositioning, and sale of over $20 million of multifamily assets in Western New York, and is now fully engaged in overseeing the end-to-end execution of the company's rapidly growing development & repositioning pipeline.

Roman holds a Bachelor's degree from the University of Toronto and an MBA in real estate management and development from the Ted Rogers School at Toronto Metropolitan University. An avid outdoorsman and a lifelong basketball fan, Roman and his family split their time between Canada and the US.

Ben Friedland

Managing Partner & Co-Founder

A true entrepreneur, Ben has 25 years of experience successfully developing comprehensive real estate platforms from inception to execution across Canada, the US, and the UK. His expertise spans multiple asset classes, but his focus has always been on value-add multifamily. Prior to co-founding 18 North, Ben served as President at Gold Wynn, where he was the growth catalyst for a $1.1 billion family office with commercial and multifamily assets in the US and UK. In this capacity, Ben also built and managed a 140-person team operating across two continents and five markets.

Ben's achievements include assembling a 6,000-unit multifamily portfolio across the US valued at more than $750 million, with 2,000 units and $120 million under management in Oklahoma. Ben also led market entry into the UK, establishing an apartment portfolio valued at $340 million. At 18 North, Ben touches all aspects of the business with particular focus on business development, strategy, and organizational design. Most recently, Ben was instrumental in getting top value for the company's now-sold Western New York portfolio, and in securing attractive off-market deals in both Tulsa and Oklahoma City.

Ben holds a BA from the University of Toronto and an MBA from the Ted Rogers School at Toronto Metropolitan University. A world traveler and family man, Ben resides in Toronto with his wife and two daughters.

Chris Brown

Managing Partner

Based in Oklahoma, Chris is an accomplished entrepreneur with a distinguished track record across multiple industries. Serving as one of the Managing Partners of 18 North, he leads an executive team combining many decades of industry experience with over $5 billion of deal structuring, as well as construction oversight and development. Chris's broader leadership experience extends to the logistics and energy sectors, serving as CEO and principal of multiple corporations strategically focused on transporting hazardous materials — including NGLs, hydrocarbon propellants, and crude oil — in major oil and gas formations including the Permian and Bakken basins, generating annual revenues of $75 million with a team of 120+ employees while establishing a reputation for safety, logistical efficiency, and operational excellence.

Chris's other ventures include a diverse portfolio of real estate holding companies with multifamily, commercial, and industrial assets across the Southern United States, including Texas, Tennessee, and Oklahoma. His entrepreneurial vision and expertise span the real estate cycle, including property acquisition, construction, debt structure, management, and asset disposition. Highlights include modernizing and stabilizing assets, leading new urbanism projects, and creating innovative multi-use spaces, making him a key figure in the industry.

Chris holds a BSB in Finance and Management from the University of Kansas. He lives in Owasso, Oklahoma, with his wife and two children.

Department Leadership

Josh Wynn

Sr. Partner, Head of Investments, Debt & Analytics

Josh is a highly skilled real estate professional with deep, hands-on experience across the full lifecycle of real estate investing, with a primary focus on multifamily. Raised in a family apartment business, he has worked in nearly every facet of the industry — from trades, leasing, and property management to brokerage and real estate law — and has since built and scaled businesses across Canadian student housing and U.S. value-add multifamily. Most recently, Josh served as Director of Finance at Gold Wynn, a Bahamas-based real estate private equity firm with a portfolio exceeding $1.5 billion, where he oversaw more than $250 million in acquisitions, dispositions, and financings. As Partner at 18 North, Josh leads investment strategy, debt origination, underwriting, and analytics. Josh holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Real Estate Finance, a law degree from the University of Western Ontario, and is a member of the Ontario Bar; he lives in Toronto with his family.

Sean Davis

Partner, Head of Development

Sean is a seasoned real estate professional with a strong track record of leadership and execution in multifamily construction. A Tulsa native, Sean contributed to the successful development of over $400 million in multifamily housing, spanning thousands of units across Oklahoma and Arkansas. He also managed construction operations for over 1.5 million sq. ft. of industrial warehouse space, utilizing advanced steel and concrete tilt-up methods. As a Partner at 18 North, Sean was instrumental in the highly successful delivery of the Reserve on 29th development project in Mustang, Oklahoma. He currently oversees over $100 million of 18 North development projects, including the Reserve at 151st, Bixby and Tulsa Hills. With a deep understanding of ground-up development, restoration, and infrastructure work, Sean brings both field expertise and executive-level vision to every project.

Tyler Copes

Partner, Head of Construction

Tyler is a veteran construction professional with over 30 years of hands-on experience across residential, commercial, and multifamily development. Having grown up working alongside his father in the Oklahoma construction trades, Tyler developed a deep-rooted understanding of the industry from an early age. His career began as a maintenance technician and rapidly progressed to superintendent, where he gained a reputation for excellence in managing large-scale projects with precision and quality. Tyler's ability to lead complex construction efforts has made him a driving force in the Midwest real estate market. Today, as a Partner at 18 North, he plays a critical role in overseeing a construction pipeline of over $200 million comprised of both owned and third-party mixed-use developments.

Shira Brym

Managing Director, 18 North Asset Management

Shira is an accomplished asset manager, lawyer, and strategic advisor with deep expertise in corporate law, risk management, and real estate asset lifecycle oversight, bringing over a decade of experience across government, private practice, and in-house counsel roles since being called to the Ontario Bar in 2011. For the past eight years, she has served as asset manager and in-house counsel at a multinational real estate investment and management company with a portfolio exceeding $1 billion across Canada, the U.S., and the UK, where her role extends well beyond traditional legal advisory to include active asset management responsibilities. At 18 North, Shira is deeply involved in asset management, tenant relations, regulatory compliance, lease negotiations, and operational risk. She holds a Bachelor of Arts and Master of Public Policy from the University of Toronto and a Juris Doctor from Osgoode Hall Law School.
