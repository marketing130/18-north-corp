01 — Brand Overview

Minimal. Modern. Institutionally credible.

18 North Corp is a vertically integrated real estate investment, development, and management firm. The brand should feel disciplined, confident, and premium — suitable for investors, lenders, institutional partners, and development collaborators.

Professional

Strategic

04 — Logo Usage

Consistent, unaltered, always premium.

The 18 North Corp mark is used without modification. No effects, shadows, gradients, or colour changes. Always allow generous clear space around the logo and use only on approved backgrounds.

  • Use logo on white, off-white, or dark navy backgrounds

  • Re-colour or modify any elements of the mark

06 — Voice & Tone

Clear, confident, and investor-grade.

All copy should feel like it was written by someone who deeply understands the market — not a marketing team. Short sentences. No fluff. No salesy language. Performance and discipline in every word.

Clear
Not → Jargon-heavy
Every sentence earns its place. If it doesn't add meaning, it doesn't belong on the page.
Confident
Not → Overpromising
We let performance speak. No superlatives or empty claims. Data and track record do the work.
Strategic
Not → Casual
We communicate at the level of our audience — institutional partners, investors, and operators.
Minimal
Not → Wordy
Three words where ten would do. Short paragraphs. Let white space carry the weight.
Direct
Not → Hedged
We make statements, not suggestions. We say what we do and what we stand for without qualification.
Grounded
Not → Hype-driven
Every claim is rooted in real performance, real assets, and real relationships.
Write This
"Driven by data. Grounded in market insight. Built for long-term value."
Short, declarative, confident. No excess words. Reads like a firm that knows exactly what it's doing.
Not This
"We are thrilled to offer our clients truly exceptional and world-class real estate investment opportunities!"
Overly enthusiastic, vague, and lacks credibility. Exclamation marks and superlatives undermine institutional tone.
Write This
"18 North co-invests meaningful GP capital in every transaction. Our interests are aligned at every level."
Specific, transparent, and trust-building. Tells the investor something real, not a slogan.
Not This
"We are passionate about real estate and truly believe in creating amazing communities for everyone!"
Sounds like a consumer brand, not an investment firm. Passion and belief without evidence carry no weight.
07 — Imagery

Wide. Calm. Architectural.

Photography carries the visual weight of the brand. 18 North's portfolio provides strong aerial, architectural, and development imagery. Use it large, uncropped, and without heavy treatment.

Aerial — Portfolio Asset
Community Amenity
Multifamily — Aerial
Urban Infill
Ground-Up Development
Use
  • Aerial and architectural photography of actual assets
  • Wide landscape crops with generous sky or ground
  • Construction progress and development site imagery
  • Clean exteriors in good natural light
  • Before/after comparisons in investment case studies
  • Minimal or no text overlays
Avoid
  • Generic business handshake or lifestyle stock photography
  • Heavy filters, vignetting, or dramatic colour grading
  • Busy collages or grid arrangements of small photos
  • Overused city skyline imagery unrelated to portfolio
  • Rounded corners beyond 4–8px
  • Drop shadows on photography
08 — Layout & Spacing

Space is not empty — it's structural.

The layout system is built on a 12-column grid with generous section padding. White space is intentional and load-bearing. Never fill space for the sake of it.

01
Full-Width Sections
All sections span the full browser width. Content is constrained by the inner container, never the section itself.
02
Thin Borders Over Cards
Prefer 1px border treatment over boxed card shadows. Borders feel editorial and restrained. Heavy shadows feel dated.
03
Alternating Backgrounds
White and off-white sections alternate to create visual rhythm without colour changes. Dark sections are used sparingly for impact.
