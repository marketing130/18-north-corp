Overview

18 North Shared Services provides the centralized operational backbone that supports all of our verticals—Development Corp, Asset Management, General Partner, and emerging entities. This team delivers high-quality shared services across key non-core functions such as finance, marketing, human resources, and technology, enabling each vertical to focus on its core competencies while operating with efficiency and cohesion.

Capabilities

Main capabilities include:

01 Bookkeeping & Financial Reporting

Accurate, centralized accounting across project and entity levels.

02 Marketing & Communications

Brand strategy, leasing materials, investor communications, and digital presence.

03 Technology & Systems

Software implementation, systems integration, and operational tech stack management.

04 Human Resources

Talent acquisition, onboarding, compliance, and team development.

Our Impact

Consistency across the platform

By pooling expertise and resources, Shared Services eliminates duplication and drives consistency across the platform. Whether supporting development budgeting, lease-up campaigns, investor reporting, or corporate governance, this vertical ensures that every part of 18 North performs at its highest potential.

It is through Shared Services that our integrated model becomes scalable, enabling each business unit to operate with agility while benefiting from enterprise-level support.
