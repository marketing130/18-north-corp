About 18 North Corp

Built to grow, develop, and manage — under one roof

18 North Corp is a rapidly growing, privately held, vertically integrated multifamily real estate company specializing in value-add acquisitions and ground-up development. Our platform — spanning development, asset management, general partner services, and shared services — means we control quality and performance at every stage of the asset lifecycle, with $100M+ currently under management.

Driven by expertise, integrity, and innovation, we are grounded in market insight and built for long-term value. We target high-growth markets where deep local relationships and operational discipline create meaningful advantages, and we're committed to building lasting relationships that benefit our partners and communities.

Development & Construction

18 North Development Corporation
