About 18 North Corp

Built to invest, develop, and manage — under one roof

18 North Corp is a privately held real estate investment and operating company specializing in value-add multifamily acquisitions and ground-up development. Our vertically integrated platform — spanning development, asset management, general partner services, and shared services — means we control quality and performance at every stage of the investment lifecycle.

Driven by data. Grounded in market insight. Built for long-term value. We target high-growth markets where deep local relationships and operational discipline create meaningful advantages for our investors and communities.
