Overview

At the center of 18 North's investment ecosystem, 18 North Capital leads the sourcing, structuring, and execution of all real estate investments. As sponsor and fiduciary, the Capital team is responsible for identifying opportunities, raising capital, and establishing deal architecture—while also taking an equity stake in each project to align our interests with those of our investors. Our commitment to co-investment is a core value: we treat our partners' capital with the same care and diligence as our own.

Capital works in close coordination with 18 North Development Corp to manage acquisitions, construction, and repositioning, and with 18 North Asset Management to oversee performance through stabilization and exit. This seamless handoff between business lines ensures end-to-end control, accountability, and value creation.

Investment Strategy

Our investment strategy is built on:

  • Acquiring off-market, value-add multifamily properties with strong fundamentals
  • Negotiating favorable acquisition and financing terms
  • Rapidly repositioning assets through targeted capital investment
  • Implementing progressive leasing strategies to drive NOI
  • Refinancing or disposing of assets in line with partner objectives

With a hands-on approach to every phase of the investment cycle, 18 North Capital translates opportunity into lasting performance—bridging investor capital with institutional-grade execution.
