Who We Are

18 North is a vertically integrated developer, owner, and operator of high-potential land and real estate assets, with a primary focus on the acquisition, development, and management of high-quality multifamily residential communities.

Our Story

18 North was founded by two long-term business partners and real estate professionals in response to growing demand for a disciplined, metrics-based approach to multifamily investment. Their combined expertise – spanning international markets, asset classes, and transaction structures – is reflected in the caliber of projects 18 North undertakes and the strategic partnerships it forms.

Where We Work

Oklahoma & Northwest Arkansas

We concentrate on select high-growth markets where we have deep local relationships and operational expertise, allowing us to make faster decisions, execute with greater discipline, and create stronger outcomes for our investors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Our Integrated Platform

Corporate Structure

Driven by data, grounded in local market insight, and guided by operational excellence, we target both value-add and ground-up development opportunities in high-growth markets. Our integrated platform – comprised of four specialized business lines: 18 North Development Corp, 18 North Asset Management, 18 North Capital, and 18 North Shared Services – enables us to align strategy and execution across every phase of a project, from sourcing to stabilization.

Together, these business lines provide full-cycle control over development, asset management, operations, and investor relations. This integration ensures that every decision is made with the long-term performance of the asset – and the strength of our communities – in mind.
